E.2 Diacritics
Diacritics are used in the script:
• U+0300 COMBINING GRAVE ACCENT represents falling tone
• U+0301 COMBINING ACUTE ACCENT represents rising tone
• U+0307 COMBINING DOT ABOVE represents stress
• U+0304 COMBINING MACRON represents +ATR (Advanced Tongue Root)
